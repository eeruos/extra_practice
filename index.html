Chess world Champions

Since 1886, there have been 17 undisputed World Champions of chess.

  1. Wilhelm Steiniz (1836-1900), World Champion 1886-1894
  2. Emanuel Lasker (1868-1941), World Champion 1894-1921
  3. José Raúl Capablanca (1888-1942), World Champion 1921-1927
  4. Alexander_Alekhine (1892-1946), World Champion 1927-1935 and 1937-1946
  5. Max Euwe (1901-1981), World Champion 1935-1937
  6. Mikhail Botvinnik (1911-1995), World Champion 1948-1957, 1958-1960, and 1961-1963
  7. Vasily Smyslov (1921-2010), World Champion 1957-1958
  8. Mikhail Tal (1936-1992), World Champion 1960-1961
  9. Tigran Petrosian (1929-1984), World Champion 1963-1969
  10. Boris Spassky (1937-), World Champion 1969-1972
  11. Bobby Fischer (1943-2008), World Champion 1972-1975
  12. Anatoly Karpov (1951-), World Champion 1975-1985
  13. Garry Kasparov (1963-), World Champion 1985-2000
  14. Vladimir Kramnik (1975-), World Champion 2000-2007
  15. Viswanathan Anand (1969-), World Champion 2007-2013
  16. Magnus Carlsen (1990-), World Champion 2013-2023
  17. Ding Liren (1992-), World Champion 2023-
